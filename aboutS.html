What is Spectra?

SPECTRA is an online media platform set on changing how women and people who identify with womanhood are represented in the media. Our Mission is 'Bringing You Women in Colours'. We plan to bring colour and dimension to stories of women in all shapes and forms. We want to be a community where women can access story-driven content that celebrates and reports stories of women around the world.
